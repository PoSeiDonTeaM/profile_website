About DENGpov

At summer of 2017, me and my childhood friend, Menelaos (seen in photo on the right) decided to create a series of mobile applications (starting with some mobile games.), so we created DENGpov. During that period, it was really intense, because we wanted to make the most difficult game ever created, in order to attract more people by using the factor of competitiveness. After long hours of no sleep , we managed to release 2 mobile games in both App Store and Play Store, called Grayscale and Flamingo Run. In addition to this, before the release we decided to launch another app, Where did I park? , whose purpose is to locate your parked car at any time using Google maps and GPS and to tell the truth? This app helped a LOT of people find their cars, especially at night. So, stay tuned for more apps and games.
